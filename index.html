Niagara Nutrition Partners - 2023
Rebranding

Who are they?


Founded in 1998

Not-for-profit

Student nutrition: elementary, secondary, community-based.

What do they do?


Maximise healthy youth development.

Support sustainable nutrition programs.

Involve community partners.

Client's Demand:



Keep up with modern trend.

Appeal to children and parents.

Active. Friendly. Educational.

Challenges:


Appeal to two age groups.

Direct donation call-to-actions prohibited.

Design Solution:



Cleaned up overlapping colors.

Dynamic fruit sizes and shine opacity to give depth to illustration.

Bolder typeface with round edge to demonstrate friendly appeal.

Slight curve to the text line recalls to a smile.

White border to hint at badge design, gives sense of community and appreciation.

Vindicated LLC - 2022
Logo Design

Who are they?


Founded in 2022.

Fashion Company.

Streetwear for young adults.

What are their themes?


Strength. Growth. Change.
Community. Connections.

Versatility in design.

Blend casual & classy fasion.

Client's Demand


Typographic Logo

Reflect Brand's theme.

Homage to skating culture.

Challenges:


Lean into established design directions, i.e. Louis Vuitton, Supreme, Addidas, without appearing as a cheap alternative.

Balance between freestyle & punk aesthetic and classy & high-end appearance.

Design Solution


Geometric Sans-serif
Borders
Vertical Stripes
=> Structure, Order & Authority.

Slight distortion effect.
Backward E.
Tilted upward angle.
=> Energy, Dynamism, Growth.

Closed Shape
=> Shield/Bar > Protection.
=> Skateboard > Ollie.
